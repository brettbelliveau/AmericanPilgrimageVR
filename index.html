Project Description

Our scene is a VR experience taking place during the Westward expansion of the 1800s. The user is given a glimpse of life on the Oregon Trail, riding by wagon with a small group of travelers.

Learning Objective

After the VR experience, participants will have a deeper understanding of the hardship and sacrifices made by travelers on the Oregon Trail.

Technical Elements

Many elements in this project were designed to increase the user's sense of presence. These major elements included:

  • First-Person Character View
  • Dialogue
  • Ambient Sounds
